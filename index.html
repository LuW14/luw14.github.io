Hi. I'm Lu Wang.

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Department of Industrial Engineering at Tsinghua University. I got my bachelor degree (2014-2018) at the Department of Industrial Engineering at Tsinghua University.

Here's my research interest.

My research interests include digital transformation strategies and implementations.

Explainable Optimization

We study how to explain optimization models, algorithms, decisions effectively and intuitively to end-users to build trust and ensure the consistency between decision and execution.

Digital Twin

We summarize four key ingredients and three challenges from OR perspective to build up efficient and intelligent Digital Twin and clear the confusion due to the likely hype associated with the concept.

Reinforcement Learning

We use MCTS algorithm to efficiently solve the optimal production ramp-up planning that jointly considers the effect of production ramp-up, nonzero leadtime and nonstationary demand.
